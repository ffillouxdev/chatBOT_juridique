Cookies Policy

At LawTchat, we use cookies to improve the experience of our users on our website containing a legal chatbot. This cookie policy explains how we use cookies and how you can control them.


What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to improve the efficiency of the site, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How do we use cookies?
When you use our website, we may place cookies on your device. These cookies help us track your preferences, analyze site usage and personalize content to provide you with a more relevant experience.

Control cookies
You can control and manage cookies according to your preferences. You can configure your browser to block cookies, delete existing cookies or warn you before a cookie is stored on your device. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain functionality of our website.


By using our website containing the legal chatbot, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our use of cookies, please contact us at contactlawtchat@gmail.com.
